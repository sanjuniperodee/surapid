SURAPID Elevator Co, Ltd is a subsidiary of New Xio Elevator Group specializing in complete solutions for elevators.

OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

New Xio Elevator is one of the top 500 enterprises in China, is the number 1 elevator integrated group in China, the largest supplier of elevator and escalator components in China. In 1997, New Xio Elevator and OTIS set up a well-known joint venture for the production of liftoffs in Hangzhou, known as XIZI OTIS.

Traction machines, control systems, door drives, safety parts - all of this we produce ourselves, which ensures the quality and reliability of our elevators and escalators.

Our headquarters is located in Hangzhou Lin'an Industrial Development Zone, covering an area of 200,000 square meters, combined with Zhejiang University Research and Development Institute, Elevator R&D Center, 208m Test Tower, 50,000m2 Production Area, etc.
